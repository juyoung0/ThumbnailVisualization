Introduction

Your faithful participation will help us establish a theory about good practices in using visualizations for data-driven journalism.
The goal of this experiment is to evaluate thumbnails designed for news articles.

This experiment is confirmed by IRB (UNISTIRB-18-52-C). Click the button below to see the IRB approval document.


Please acknowledge the following restrictions before participating in our experiment. If you do not satisfy these restrictions, you will NOT receive compensation for your participation. Please read them carefully.

  • You must use a display with a sufficiently high resolution (minimum 1024 X 768). If your display has an insufficient display resolution, you may not proceed.
  • You can use a personal desktop or laptop computer. Participation with tablets, smart phones, or other mobile devices is not permitted.
  • You may be asked to maximize your browser window if its initial size is insufficient for participation.

Tutorial

  1. In this experiment, you will be asked to read two articles about public trust and stock prices and to evaluate 17 thumbnails per article (34 thumbnails in total).
  2. When you enter the experiment, you will be asked to fill out a demographic survey.
  3. When you complete the survey, you will be asked to read the first article in three minutes and to solve a simple question about the article. A simple question can be solved by reading all the charts and contents of the article, so please read the article thoroughly. (IF YOU ANSWER WRONG, YOU WILL NOT RECEIVE COMPENSATION.)
  4. Once you pass the simple question, you will be asked to evaluate 17 thumbnails for the article you read.
  5. You will be asked to provide your responses to five questions per article.
  6. For evaluation, you need to provide your responses as scores ranging from 1 to 7 with 7 being the highest score.
  7. You will be asked to repeat c. to f. for the second article.
  8. Once you complete the evaluation tasks, you will be given a token number on the screen. You need to keep the token number and enter it to the HIT on Amazon Mechanical Turk to get paid.
